ReCircle ♻️
AI-Powered Circular Commerce Platform for Sustainable Returns, Resale & Product Lifecycle Management

Transforming product returns from a cost center into a value-generating circular economy ecosystem.

🚀 Overview

ReCircle is an AI-powered Circular Commerce Platform built for HackOn with Amazon 2026 under the theme:

"Second Life Commerce: AI-Powered Returns & Sustainable Resale"

Every year millions of returned products are discarded, recycled prematurely, or sold through unverified channels despite remaining functional. ReCircle solves this problem by intelligently analyzing returned products, assigning them a digital identity, predicting resale value, routing them to the most suitable second-life destination, and tracking their environmental impact.

Instead of treating returns as waste, ReCircle transforms them into opportunities for:

Resale
Refurbishment
Donation
Recycling
Sustainability Reporting
🎯 Problem Statement

E-commerce returns create massive economic and environmental challenges:

Billions of dollars lost annually through returned inventory.
Growing landfill waste from usable products.
Lack of transparency in refurbished and second-hand products.
High reverse logistics costs.
Return fraud and inefficient product routing.
Limited visibility into a product's lifecycle and sustainability impact.

As online commerce grows, these challenges become increasingly difficult to manage at scale.

💡 Our Solution

ReCircle introduces an AI-driven ecosystem that evaluates every returned product and determines its most valuable and sustainable next step.

The platform combines:

AI Product Grading
Smart Routing
Product Passports
Return Prevention
Donation Network Integration
Sustainability Analytics
Dynamic Resale Price Prediction

to maximize product recovery value while minimizing waste.
